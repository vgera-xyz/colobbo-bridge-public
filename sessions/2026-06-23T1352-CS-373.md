## consumed_fields grounding (applying the inc-2 convention)

Per the convention set in inc-2 (**declare the domain fields you read; the ADR-007 envelope is not declared**), the field-lists were grounded against the actual Mobile LOBE.md methodology — not guessed:
- **From ArchResult:** `components` (Step 2-3 filter/expand) + `interface_contracts` (Step 4 opaque pass-through). Same two as Frontend.
- **From BackendResult: ONLY `components`** — `components[].persistence_ops[].table_or_sp` (Steps 5-6 persistence/sync specs) + `backend_component_id` matching (Step 3). Mobile does **NOT** read `BackendResult.interface_contracts` — its own `interface_contracts` pass through from ArchResult, not Backend (LOBE.md §Step 4). This is the multi-upstream nuance: each upstream gets its own field-list reflecting what that consumer actually reads from *it*.

Both field-lists δ-valid (every field ∈ the respective producer's output-schema `.properties`).
