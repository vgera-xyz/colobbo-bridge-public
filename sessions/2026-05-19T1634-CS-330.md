concern). **Empirically tested:** `git log --follow
agents/global/backend-lobe-codegen.md` = 1 commit (CS-327), never modified
by the CS-326/CS-328 ships — each sibling consumes only its own

orchestrator (`cibo-pipeline` hardcodes the chain; `_registry.json`

trajectory (orchestration-architecture.md Pattern A→A.5→B; Guardrail #3
forbids the leapfrog, gated on the ADR-012 capability-profile migration).

**CS-352** (Low, Backlog) files the Pattern-B registry-declared-consumption
retrofit as tracked Phase-2 future work. Glaze block mirrors siblings
byte-symmetrically (NO `consumes` field — lands fleet-wide in the
Phase-2 retrofit, preserving ADR-031 §1.D6 sibling-Ware symmetry).
